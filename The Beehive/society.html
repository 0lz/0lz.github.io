Honey bee society

Social order

The society can be broken up into 4 different classes. There is the queen bee, the nurses, the foragers and the drones. The queen bee is the bee that lays the eggs for the drones to fertilize. The drones are male bees that do all sorts of tasks. They can for example be workers or forager. Nurses are female bees who take care of the eggs, the larvae and the queen. After a couple of weeks the nurses change into foragers who go out to find nectar and pollen although this can vary because they can also be workers.
