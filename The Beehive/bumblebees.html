Bumblebees

This page will explore some of the differences between the 2 most famous types of bees; bumble- and honeybees
these two are pictured side by side:

You'll probably notice a couple of differences between the 2 species. The biggest ones are outlined in the table below:

Honey Bee Bumblebee
Shape Long Round
Skin Smooth Hairy
Die when stinging Yes No
Wings Transparent Dark
Nests Beehives Underground
