Natural enemies (and how to deal with them)

threats

Birds, spiders and parasites are all enemies of bees. There are easy and simple ways to stop these predators from damaging your hives.


How to repel them

For parasites you can use insecticides. This may kill some of your bees but the venom will kill less bees than the parasites. A good hive can protect the bees from birds, toads or any big predators. Regularly cleaning around your hives will stop spiders from making webs that catch bees.
