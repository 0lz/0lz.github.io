Evolution of honey bees

cooperation

honey bees were able to develop such complex societies, because the genetic makeup of each bee in a beehive is the same. This means it's in the evolutionary interest of the bees to cooperate.

flowers

fossil records show bees coevolved with flowers; Throughout the generations bees and flowers became more and more dependent on one another. Today flowers and bees can't survive without one another.
