Bee mimics

Because bees sting its in the evolutionary interest of insects to look like bees. As a result there are many insects that look like bees which aren't actually bees. These insects can be totally unrelated to actual bees. Examples include flies, butterflies and moths. This page includes several images of these 'mimics'
