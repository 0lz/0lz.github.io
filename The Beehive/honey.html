Honey production

Honey is of course the product bees make. It’s your reward for protecting your bees. It’s tasty and can be put in drinks like tea. Honey also has a practically infinite shelf life: it will never go stale. Bees make honey as ration for the winter. When winter comes and there are no flowers they depend on honey to survive, so make sure you don’t overharvest.
