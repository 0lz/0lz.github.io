Beekeeping

Beekeeping is a very delicate hobby. It takes a lot of energy and micromanagement. As a beginner it is smart to seek out local beekeepers who are willing to help you starting up. We recommend that you help out a beekeeper in his daily activities before starting your own colony. This way you can find out if you like the hobby without commiting yourself to it
